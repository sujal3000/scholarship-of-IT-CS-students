Key recommendations to improve your chances

  • Track deadlines closely: Most scholarships open once a year with strict cutoffs.
  • Prepare documents early: Income certificate, caste/minority certificate (if applicable), Class 12 marksheet, entrance exam rank, disability certificate (for Saksham), etc.
  • Diversify applications: Apply to both corporate and government scholarships to maximize chances.
  • Check portals weekly: Buddy4Study, Vidyasaarathi, and NSP update frequently.
